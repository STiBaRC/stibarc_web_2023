Account Visibility

Making your account private will:
  • Retain existing followers
  • Require new followers to be approved
  • Make new posts private by default (you may still make public posts)
You may choose to keep your existing posts' visibility, or make them all private.
 

Account Visibility

Making your account public will:
  • Make your account visible to everyone
  • Allow anyone to follow you without approval
  • Make all new posts public by default (you may still make private posts)
Keep in mind that your private posts will easily be visible as anyone can follow your account.

You may choose to keep your existing posts' visibility, or make them all public.
